Rentals, Sales, & Repairs

Willoe Rentals is a locally owned and operated tool & equipment rental company that has served the needs of contractors, farmers, and homeowners in Stratford and the surrounding areas of Southwestern Ontario since 2003.

Equipment Rentals & Sales

Willoe Rentals is proud to be an authorized dealer of Stihl outdoor power equipment, TORO lawn mowers, & ECHO power equipment.

We also offer a wide variety of rental equipment.

Services & Repairs

We service and repair lawn & garden equipment, small engines, sharpen chainsaws, and much more.

We are always available to answer any questions you may have to ensure that you choose the correct tool to get the job done right, while saving you time and money.

Please give us a call at (519) 271-5155 or send us an e-mail to discuss your project further.
